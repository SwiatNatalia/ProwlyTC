ID.A1 Searching "Prowly" in Google graphics.

Preconditions
Opened Google.com web page

Test steps:
1. Search "prowly" in google browser
2. Click on All filters button
3. Choose Graphics filter

Expected result:
Page with pictures associated with Prowly brand is loaded.

ID.A2 Searching "Prowly" in Google Maps

Preconditions
Opened Google.com web page

Test steps:
1. Search "prowly" in google browser
2. Click on All filters button
3. Choose Map filter

Expected result:
Google map is opened. The location of Prowly's HQ is marked with the pin on the map. Information panel contains contact details, address and rating of the company.
